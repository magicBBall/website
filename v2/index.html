About Us

Magic Basketball Club is a family focussed, community minded club who welcome players of all levels of abilities, whether it be to play for fun, competitively or to make new friendships.

The club offers boys and girls’ teams from Under 8 through to Under 19’s in a junior competition. In addition to this, we offer Men’s teams in the senior competition. After all, sometimes the big kids want in on the fun too.

Magic coaches provide players and teams with opportunities to learn new skills and develop existing ones. The club also holds age specific focussed training days as well as social engagements to promote fun and bring the Magic family together. Aside from fun, Magic is big on teamwork, respect and sportsmanship.
